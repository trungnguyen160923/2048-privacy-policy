Privacy Policy – 2048 Game

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the mobile application “2048 Game” (the “App”). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Device Identifier
    Android device ID (ANDROID_ID) or a similar device identifier.
  • Profile Information
    Nickname (display name chosen by you).
    Optional status message (only when you reach top leaderboard positions).
  • Game Data
    Game scores, board size, rank, and related statistics.

We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, contacts, or precise location.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To operate the online leaderboard and show rankings between players.
  • To save your progress and high scores.
  • To detect basic cheating or abuse (for example, unusual score submissions).
  • To improve the App and understand how players interact with the game (basic analytics on our server).

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • Your data is sent to and stored on our own backend server only for the purposes described above.
  • We may use standard hosting or infrastructure providers (e.g., cloud server providers) to run our backend, but they do not use your data for their own purposes.

4. Public Information

Your nickname, score, rank, and optional status message may be displayed publicly on the in‑game leaderboard to other players. Please avoid using sensitive personal information in your nickname or status message.

5. Data Security

  • We strive to protect your data and use reasonable technical measures to secure information on our server.
  • When possible, we use encrypted connections (HTTPS) between the App and our server.

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • We retain your leaderboard and game data for as long as it is needed to operate the App and the online rankings.
  • If you would like us to delete your data (nickname, scores, status message, and related identifiers), you can contact us and we will make reasonable efforts to remove it.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is a casual puzzle game and is not specifically directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age required by local laws. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version in the App or on our website and update the “Last updated” date.
